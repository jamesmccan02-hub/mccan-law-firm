About McCan Law Firm

Leading Experts in Cryptocurrency Asset Recovery

Our Story

Founded in 2018, McCan Law Firm emerged at a critical time when cryptocurrency adoption was surging, but legal protections for investors were virtually non-existent. Our founder, James McCan, witnessed firsthand how traditional legal firms were ill-equipped to handle the complex intersection of blockchain technology, international law, and financial fraud.

What started as a small practice helping individual Bitcoin scam victims has grown into one of the most respected cryptocurrency recovery law firms globally. We've successfully recovered over $50 million in stolen crypto assets for more than 500 clients across 40 countries.

Our success stems from a unique combination: deep legal expertise, cutting-edge blockchain forensics capabilities, and strategic partnerships with cryptocurrency exchanges and law enforcement agencies worldwide.

Unlike traditional law firms, we've established direct relationships with major cryptocurrency exchanges worldwide. This enables faster freezing of stolen assets and more efficient recovery processes.

Our attorneys specialize exclusively in cryptocurrency law. We understand the technical aspects of blockchain technology and stay current with rapidly evolving regulations across multiple jurisdictions.
